City Route is an app and allows locals to build a tour around the city in which they live in for travelers who want the local experience in a city.

It allows you to purchase group tickets at a discounted price.

ParkAss

An application that reports drivers parked in a disabled parking space without a handicapped/disabled parking permit.

The app is programmed in react native using an API of Google Maps.
The reports are sent on WhatsApp to each municipality according to the location identified.
Every report that is produced and sent is saved on Firebase.

DogVille - Hotel&Spa

Won UI/UX form competition

The program presents a registration form for the future dog staying at a dog boarding house.
I chose to present the dog as a customer filling out the form.
The content of the form is humorous and addresses the dog himself. When you click on the dog, it lengthens according to the content (using jquery) and a new field appears for filling (JavaScript functions).
